Product

Built for the way hospitals actually run.

No-shows are expensive. Front-desk staff calling every patient by hand doesn't scale. Vaani does the calling — properly, patiently, in the patient's own language.

Bilingual by default

Detects Hindi, English, or a mix mid-sentence, and always replies in the language the patient just used — no configuration needed.

Confirms, reschedules, cancels

Every call ends with a clear outcome, captured automatically.

Consent-first, every call

Real phone calls

Not an app, not a chatbot widget — an actual outbound call to the number already on file, no new hardware or phone lines required.

Outcomes, tracked
